Introduction

Teaching is one of the hardest jobs that we could ever do in our life.We are molding a child’s character, intellectual, emotional and apply the learning in life.
In practice teaching, future teachers are being trained first to become effective and efficient, letting them experience what are waiting ahead. It never been easy,
future teachers need to become flexible. Need to know how to manage their time.Need to strive hard, to practice and strengthen their patients,to build their character.
The school need to prepare them for the life of being a teacher.

Retrospection

As I think about my experience from the very beginning when reality hit me so hard about me being a teacher one day, I can say that it’s too hard, it needs patients, it needs perseverance, and it needs passions to keep on the field I am taking. I learned so much things, how to treat everyone as a family, how to strive hard to build the character standard of being a teacher, how to think wisely before making a decision. All of those make sense when I enrolled the practice teaching subject. I find it so hard to have a practice teaching online. It is very hard to build a relationship to the students of your cooperating teacher. The new normal literally makes me exhausted and drained but also got my attention to the challenge it offers me. It taught me about the things should do in e-learning. It has a big difference in having face-to-face class. I learned so many things about being a teacher from the encouragement of Ma’am Rhismane Montejo where telling us to enjoy the process to become a good teacher, Doc. Jonel Victoria where his word strike me and make me realize that the voice of the teacher is very important. It makes me conscious very time. It helps me to take care of my voice because I need it as long as I teach. I am really grateful. I am also thankful to my cooperating teacher, Sir Arci Manangan for always giving his support and give me advice how to handle students, though sometimes I am struggling because I did not meet his expectation but he keeps on encouraging me to continue and learn from my experience. I never forget the time where he said that I need to build a humoristic characteristic in me because it gives big impact to students, he encourage me to step out of my comfort zone and start practicing humor. I really appreciate these three people who never give up on helping me to improve as I am taking my journey as a practice teacher.
